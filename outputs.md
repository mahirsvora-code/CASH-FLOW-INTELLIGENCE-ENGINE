# Outputs

## Primary outputs
- Monthly closing cash balance
- First negative cash month
- Cash runway length
- Funding gap amount

## Decision outputs
- Go / caution / risk status
- Suggested fix for liquidity pressure
- Delay impact summary

## Visual outputs
- Monthly cash curve
- Inflow and outflow comparison
- Scenario comparison table
- Alert panel for funding gaps

## Plain-language insights
- You will run out of cash in Month X
- Delay of X months increases pressure by Y
- Changing payment timing reduces the gap by Z
