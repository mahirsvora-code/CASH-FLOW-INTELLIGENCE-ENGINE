# User Pain Points

## Developer pain
- Cash comes in later than expected
- Payments to contractors and materials come earlier than planned
- Interest keeps running even when sales are slow
- Project looks profitable but still runs out of cash

## Consultant pain
- Hard to explain liquidity pressure to founders
- Difficult to show how delay changes project viability
- Need a simple output that supports decision-making

## Investor pain
- Need to know whether the project can survive without constant extra funding
- Need to see timing risk, not only headline profit
